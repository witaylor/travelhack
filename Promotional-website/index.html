What is TravelHack?

TravelHack is a hackathon for undergrads, run completely by current Expedia Group interns. We want to encourage people from all years and of all experiences to apply for the hackathon if you're passionate about tech and travel - but especially if you're interested in a placement year. Make sure to register your interest in a placement and you'll be at the top of the entry list. We can't wait to see you in November!

How do I apply?

Pro Tip!

Apply with a friend - it'll be one less person you need to get to know.

You don't have to be a coding genius to participate! TravelHack is designed to welcome people from many varying backgrounds with different tech knowledge. All you need to do is complete a short take-home coding test. We challenge you to come along and share your experience and work with new people to create something cool, and probably learn loads along the way!
